# KADOSH (קָדוֹשׁ)

#### Standard Meaning:

"Holy," "Sanctified," "Set Apart." Usually interpreted as "morally superior" or "religiously pious."

#### Hebraic Meaning:

Fundamentally, this means **"Cut Separate"** or **"Distinct."** It refers to **Ontological Otherness**. In this framework, _Kadosh_ is **Frequency Compatibility**. It defines the specific energetic resonance required to house the Infinite _Yesh_. God is _Kadosh_ (The Source, distinct from Creation). For the finite vessel (Man) to house God, it must be tuned to the same frequency. It is the **Gold Overlay** on the Acacia Wood. The wood (Humanity) is common; the Gold (Divinity) is _Kadosh_. The command "Be Holy" is the instruction to **Insulate the Vessel**.

#### Paleo-Hebrew Linguistics:


- **Qof (ק):** Sun on Horizon—Circle, Cycle, Holiness, Gather.
    
- **Dalet (ד):** Door—Pathway, Entrance.
    
- **Vav (ו):** Nail—Connector, Hook.
    
- **Shin (ש):** Teeth—Consume, Destroy, Press, Fire.
    

#### The Picture:

The **DIVINE CIRCUIT (Qof)** opening the **DOOR (Dalet)** to the **CONSUMING FIRE (Shin)**.Or: The **CYCLE (Qof)** connected (Vav) to the **DOOR (Dalet)** of the **FIRE (Shin)**._Note:_ Holiness is intimately connected to Fire/Voltage. It is the gateway to the Consuming Fire.
