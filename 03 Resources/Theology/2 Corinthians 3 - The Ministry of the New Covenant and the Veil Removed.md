# 2 Corinthians 3 - The Ministry of the New Covenant and the Veil Removed

This is where Paul **EXPLODES** the old wineskin.

Chapter 3 is the **HINGE** of the entire letter ‚Äî where Paul contrasts:

- **Old Covenant** (letter, stone, death, condemnation, fading glory)
- **New Covenant** (Spirit, heart, life, righteousness, increasing glory)

But through the **Yesh-Echad framework**, we're going to see something **DEEPER** than a simple "Old vs. New" comparison.

We're going to see:

- **The Architecture of Identity** (you ARE the letter)
- **The Veil as Consciousness Shift** (not physical but perceptual)
- **The Mirror Theology** (beholding transforms)
- **The Progressive Unveiling** (from glory to glory)

Let's go.

## **2 CORINTHIANS 3:1-3 ‚Äî YOU ARE THE LETTER**

> "Are we beginning to commend ourselves again? Or do we need, as some do, letters of recommendation to you, or from you? You yourselves are our letter of recommendation, written on our hearts, to be known and read by all. And you show that you are a letter from Christ delivered by us, written not with ink but with the Spirit of the living God, not on tablets of stone but on tablets of human hearts."

### **The TSELEM (◊¶÷∂◊ú÷∂◊ù) Principle**

Paul says: **"You ARE the letter."**

Not "I wrote you a letter." Not "You received a letter."

**YOU ARE THE LETTER.**

This is **TSELEM** theology ‚Äî **Image/Manifestation**:

**Paleo-Hebrew of TSELEM (◊¶÷∂◊ú÷∂◊ù):**

- **Tzade (◊¶):** Fishhook, Side ‚Äî To Capture, Pull Toward
- **Lamed (◊ú):** Shepherd's Staff ‚Äî Authority, Direction
- **Mem (◊ù):** Water, Chaos, Blood

**Picture:** CAPTURING (Tzade) the AUTHORITY (Lamed) in the CHAOS/BLOOD (Mem)

**You are the PHYSICAL MANIFESTATION of the INVISIBLE GOD.**

**The Corinthians aren't just "people Paul ministered to."** **They are LIVING EPISTLES ‚Äî walking, breathing LETTERS testifying to the reality of Christ.**

### **The Three Tablets**

Paul contrasts **THREE TABLETS**:

|**Tablet**|**Material**|**Written By**|**Location**|**Result**|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**OLD**|Stone|Finger of God|External (Sinai)|Death (letter kills)|
|**TRANSITION**|Ink/Papyrus|Paul's pen|External (paper)|Temporary|
|**NEW**|Flesh/Heart|Spirit of God|Internal (heart)|Life (Spirit gives life)|

**The progression:**

1. **Stone** ‚Äî External, hard, unchanging, breakable
2. **Ink** ‚Äî External, temporary, can fade
3. **Heart** ‚Äî Internal, living, permanent, transforming

### **Written on Our Hearts**

Paul says the Corinthians are written **"on our hearts"** (Paul's heart) AND **"on tablets of human hearts"** (their own hearts).

This is the **FRACTAL ECHAD** pattern again:

**God ‚Üí Paul ‚Üí Corinthians ‚Üí World**

- God writes on Paul's heart
- Paul's ministry writes on the Corinthians' hearts
- The Corinthians' lives write on the world's heart

**ONE LETTER (ECHAD), multiple expressions.**

### **Not with Ink, But with the Spirit**

**"Written not with ink but with the Spirit of the living God."**

This is **MONERGISM** ‚Äî God doing the writing, not human effort.

**RUACH (◊®◊ï÷º◊ó÷∑)** ‚Äî Spirit:

- **Resh (◊®):** Head, Master, Ruler
- **Vav (◊ï):** Nail, Hook, Connector
- **Chet (◊ó):** Fence, Inner Room, Sanctuary

**Picture:** The MASTER (Resh) who HOOKS (Vav) us to the SANCTUARY (Chet)

The Spirit is the **DIVINE SCRIBE** writing the **DABAR (◊ì÷∏÷º◊ë÷∏◊®)** ‚Äî the Living Word ‚Äî directly onto the **RECHEM (◊®÷∂◊ó÷∂◊ù)** ‚Äî the womb/heart.

**This is not external compliance.** **This is internal transformation.**

**The Torah is no longer "out there" on stone.** **The Torah is "in here" ‚Äî written on the fleshy tablets of the heart.**

This fulfills **Jeremiah 31:33**:

> "I will put my law within them, and I will write it on their hearts."

### **Letter vs. Spirit**

**NOT:**

## **2 CORINTHIANS 3:7-11 ‚Äî THE FADING VS. THE PERMANENT**

> "Now if the ministry of death, carved in letters on stone, came with such glory that the Israelites could not gaze at Moses' face because of its glory, which was being brought to an end, will not the ministry of the Spirit have even more glory? For if there was glory in the ministry of condemnation, the ministry of righteousness must far exceed it in glory. Indeed, in this case, what once had glory has come to have no glory at all, because of the glory that surpasses it. For if what was being brought to an end came with glory, much more will what is permanent have glory."

### **The KAVOD (◊õ÷∏÷º◊ë◊ï÷π◊ì) Gradient**

**KAVOD (◊õ÷∏÷º◊ë◊ï÷π◊ì)** ‚Äî Glory, Weight, Substance, Significance

**Paleo-Hebrew:**

- **Kaf (◊õ):** Palm, Hand ‚Äî To Cover, Hold
- **Bet (◊ë):** House ‚Äî Dwelling, Inside
- **Vav (◊ï):** Nail, Hook ‚Äî Connection
- **Dalet (◊ì):** Door ‚Äî Opening, Pathway

**Picture:** The HAND (Kaf) that secures the DWELLING (Bet) by the NAIL (Vav) at the DOOR (Dalet)

**Kavod is the SUBSTANTIAL PRESENCE of God that is so weighty it cannot be moved.**

Paul establishes a **GLORY GRADIENT**:

1. **Old Covenant Glory** (KAVOD on Moses' face)
    
    - **Real** (not fake)
    - **Visible** (Israelites saw it)
    - **Temporary** (fading, being brought to an end)
    - **External** (on Moses' face, not in their hearts)
2. **New Covenant Glory** (KAVOD in believers' hearts)
    
    - **Real** (same source ‚Äî God)
    - **Invisible to physical eyes** (but discernible by Spirit)
    - **Permanent** (not fading, increasing)
    - **Internal** (written on hearts, transforming from within)

### **Ministry of Death vs. Ministry of Life**

Paul calls the Old Covenant **"ministry of death"** and **"ministry of condemnation."**

**This sounds harsh. What does he mean?**

**The Old Covenant:**

- Revealed the STANDARD (holiness, righteousness)
- Diagnosed the PROBLEM (sin, separation)
- But provided NO POWER to fix it

**The diagnostic tool (Torah) was PERFECT.** **The problem was the PATIENT (humanity in the flesh).**

**Romans 7:12** ‚Äî "So the law is holy, and the commandment is holy and righteous and good."

**But:**

**Romans 8:3** ‚Äî "For God has done what the law, weakened by the flesh, could not do."

**The law was like an X-ray:**

- It shows you the broken bone
- But it doesn't heal the bone
- It just confirms: "Yes, you're broken"

**The Spirit is like the SURGEON:**

- He doesn't just DIAGNOSE
- He OPERATES
- He HEALS
- He TRANSFORMS from the inside out

### **The Fading Glory**

**"The glory which was being brought to an end."**

Moses' face glowed after being in God's presence (Exodus 34:29-35).

But **the glory FADED.**

**Why?**

Because **external glory ALWAYS fades.**

**Stone tablets break.** **Physical temples crumble.** **Outward rituals become empty.**

**But the INTERNAL KAVOD ‚Äî the Spirit writing on the heart ‚Äî INCREASES.**

**"From glory to glory"** (coming in verse 18).

## **2 CORINTHIANS 3:12-18 ‚Äî THE VEIL REMOVED**

> "Since we have such a hope, we are very bold, not like Moses, who would put a veil over his face so that the Israelites might not gaze at the outcome of what was being brought to an end. But their minds were hardened. For to this day, when they read the old covenant, that same veil remains unlifted, because only through Christ is it taken away. Yes, to this day whenever Moses is read a veil lies over their hearts. But when one turns to the Lord, the veil is removed. Now the Lord is the Spirit, and where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom. And we all, with unveiled face, beholding the glory of the Lord, are being transformed into the same image, from one degree of glory to another. For this comes from the Lord who is the Spirit."

### **The Veil Theology**

**What was the veil?**

**Physical Veil (Exodus 34:33-35):**

- Moses wore a veil AFTER speaking with God
- He removed the veil when speaking TO God
- He wore the veil when speaking to the people

**Why?**

Paul says: **"So that the Israelites might not gaze at the outcome of what was being brought to an end."**

Moses was **HIDING THE FADING.**

The glory on his face was **TEMPORARY** ‚Äî it would fade between encounters with God.

The veil prevented the people from seeing the **DIMINISHMENT** of the glory.

### **The Metaphorical Veil**

But Paul shifts from **PHYSICAL veil** (on Moses' face) to **PERCEPTUAL veil** (on the people's hearts).

**"But their minds were hardened. For to this day... that same veil remains unlifted."**

**The veil is now a CONSCIOUSNESS BARRIER.**

**What does the veil represent?**

**THE ILLUSION OF SEPARATION.**

**The veil is:**

- **HAVEL (◊î÷∂◊ë÷∂◊ú)** consciousness ‚Äî believing you're empty vapor, separated from God
- **RA (◊®÷∑◊¢)** consciousness ‚Äî the HEAD (Resh) SEEING (Ayin) for itself, ego-centered perception
- **NASHA (◊†÷∏◊©÷∏◊Å◊ê)** state ‚Äî forgetting your true position, displaced awareness

**When you read Scripture THROUGH THE VEIL:**

- You see only EXTERNAL RULES (letter)
- You feel only CONDEMNATION (you're not measuring up)
- You experience only DEATH (the killing effect of law without Spirit)

**When you read Scripture WITHOUT THE VEIL:**

- You see IDENTITY REVELATION (Spirit)
- You feel AWAKENING (you're IN CHRIST, complete in Him)
- You experience LIFE (the quickening effect of Spirit-illumined Word)

### **"Only Through Christ Is It Taken Away"**

**"That same veil remains unlifted, because only through Christ is it taken away."**

**Christ is the VEIL-REMOVER.**

**How?**

**At the Cross, the VEIL OF THE TEMPLE was TORN** (Matthew 27:51).

**Two meanings:**

**1. PHYSICAL VEIL (External)**

- The curtain separating Holy Place from Holy of Holies
- Symbolized the BARRIER between God and humanity
- Torn from **TOP TO BOTTOM** (God's action, not man's)

**2. METAPHORICAL VEIL (Internal)**

- **Hebrews 10:20** ‚Äî "The curtain, that is, his flesh"
- Christ's FLESH was the veil
- His death TORE the veil (removed the barrier)
- Now we have **BOLD ACCESS** to the throne (Hebrews 4:16)

### **The Veil Lies Over Their Hearts**

**"Yes, to this day whenever Moses is read a veil lies over their hearts."**

**This is not about Jewish people specifically.**

**This is about ANYONE who reads Scripture:**

- **FROM THE FLESH** (ego-centered, performance-based)
- **WITHOUT THE SPIRIT** (no internal illumination)
- **UNDER THE LETTER** (external rules, not internal identity)

**The veil is PERCEPTUAL BLINDNESS to:**

- Your position IN CHRIST
- Your identity as the TEMPLE
- Your status as BELOVED SON/DAUGHTER
- The FINISHED WORK of the Cross

**When you operate from HAVEL (vapor) consciousness:**

- You read "Be holy" as STRIVING (try harder)
- You read "Love your neighbor" as DUTY (force yourself)
- You read "Blessed are the pure in heart" as CONDEMNATION (you're not pure enough)

**When you operate from YESH (substance) consciousness:**

- You read "Be holy" as IDENTITY (you ARE holy in Christ)
- You read "Love your neighbor" as OVERFLOW (Christ's love through you)
- You read "Blessed are the pure in heart" as PROMISE (your heart is being purified)

### **"But When One Turns to the Lord"**

**"But when one turns to the Lord, the veil is removed."**

**The word "turns" is EPISTREPHO (·ºêœÄŒπœÉœÑœÅŒ≠œÜœâ)** ‚Äî to turn around, to return, to convert.

This is **SHUV (◊©◊Å◊ï÷º◊ë)** in Greek:

**Paleo-Hebrew of SHUV:**

- **Shin (◊©◊Å):** Teeth, Press, Fire
- **Vav (◊ï):** Connector
- **Bet (◊ë):** House

**Picture:** The PRESSING/FIRE (Shin) that SECURES (Vav) the return to the HOUSE (Bet)

**When you TURN to the Lord:**

- You shift from **EGO-CENTRIC** to **THEOS-CENTRIC**
- You move from **LETTER** to **SPIRIT**
- You transition from **HAVEL** to **YESH**
- You experience **ZAKAR (◊ñ÷∏◊õ÷∑◊®)** ‚Äî remembrance, awakening

**The veil doesn't need to be "worked off" through effort.**

**The veil is REMOVED the moment you TURN.**

**This is instantaneous ZAKAR** ‚Äî surgical remembrance ‚Äî where the RUACH uses the DABAR (Word) to CUT through the amnesia and ANOINT your consciousness with TRUTH.

### **"The Lord Is the Spirit"**

**"Now the Lord is the Spirit, and where the Spirit of the Lord is, there is freedom."**

**This is a MASSIVE theological statement.**

Paul is equating:

- **The Lord (YHWH/Christ)**

**This is ECHAD (◊ê÷∂◊ó÷∏◊ì)** ‚Äî composite unity:

- The **FATHER** (Source, hidden)

**The Spirit doesn't bring:**

## **2 CORINTHIANS 3:18 ‚Äî THE MIRROR THEOLOGY**

> "And we all, with unveiled face, beholding the glory of the Lord, are being transformed into the same image, from one degree of glory to another. For this comes from the Lord who is the Spirit."

This verse is the **THEOLOGICAL SUMMIT** of 2 Corinthians 3.

### **"We All"**

The veil is **REMOVED** (past tense, finished action).

**The veil WAS removed at the Cross.**

Because the **OLD** was **EXTERNAL** (temporary). The **NEW** is **INTERNAL** (permanent).

**The transformation is:**

- **FROM the Lord** (He's the source)

**MONERGISM + PARTICIPATION:**
