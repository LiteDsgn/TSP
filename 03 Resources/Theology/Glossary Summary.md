# Glossary Summary

Here is the comprehensive Glossary Summary of every word from your list, categorized and defined according to the **[[YESH|Yesh]]-[[ECHAD|Echad]] / Seed-Incarnation** framework we have built.

---

### THEME 1: THE NATURE OF GOD, REALITY & TIME

- **YESH (יֵשׁ):** Substance / Existence. The "Is-ness" of God. The only true Reality in the universe.
    
- **ECHAD (אֶחָד):** One / Unity. The non-dual nature of God. The reality that God and His Creation are united in one composite existence.
    
- **[[EHYEH|EHYEH]] (אֶהְיֶה):** "I AM" / "I Will Be." The Name of God as the Becoming One. The promise of Incarnation.
    
- **[[ASHER|ASHER]] (אֲשֶׁר):** "That" / "Who." The connector in the Divine Name (_Ehyeh Asher Ehyeh_). The bridge between Being and Manifestation.
    
- **[[HASHEM|HASHEM]] (הַשֵּׁם):** "The Name." The Relational Reality of God. Not a label, but a reputation and an atmosphere to inhabit.
    
- **[[EMET|EMET]] (אֱמֶת):** Truth / Faithfulness. Stability. Reality as it sees itself.
    
- **[[OLAM|OLAM]] (עוֹלָם):** Eternity / World / Hidden. The dimension outside of time; the perspective of the Whole.
    
- **[[TEMID|TEMID]] (תָּמִיד):** Perpetual / Continual. The constant, uninterrupted flow of Divine Life.
    
- **[[TOV|TOV]] (טוֹב):** Good. Functional. Aligned with the Divine purpose (Creation functioning as designed).
    

### THEME 2: THE IDENTITY OF MAN & THE VESSEL

- **[[ADAM|ADAM]] (אָדָם):** Man / Humanity. The Corporate Son. Linguistically "God (Aleph) in Blood ([[DAM|Dam]])."
    
- **[[ZERA|ZERA]] (זֶרַע):** Seed. The singular offspring (Christ) planted in the soil of humanity.
    
- **DAM (דָּם):** Blood. The soil/liquid of mortality. The carrier of the soul (_Nephesh_).
    
- **[[TSELEM|TSELEM]] (צֶלֶם):** Image / Shadow. The projection of the Invisible God into visible form.
    
- **[[BAYIT|BAYIT]] (בַּיִת):** House. The status of Humanity as the Temple/Container for God.
    
- **[[KELI|KELI]] (כְּלִי):** Vessel / Tool. The capacity of the human form to hold the Divine Substance.
    
- **[[PANIM|PANIM]] (פָּנִים):** Face / Presence. The interface of relationship.
    
- **[[EVED|EVED]] (עֶבֶד):** Servant / Worker. God as the sole Worker (Monergism) in salvation.
    

### THEME 3: THE GARDEN CYCLE (SOWING & PRESSURE)

- **[[EDEN|EDEN]] (עֵדֶן):** Pleasure / Door. The state of pure Sight/Union. The destination of the Seed.
    
- **[[MAVET|MAVET]] (מָוֶת):** Death. The Planting Phase. The necessary dissolution of the Shell so the Seed can live.
    
- **[[KERUVIM|KERUVIM]] (כְּרֻבִים):** Cherubim. The "Great Pressers." The Soil Pressure that ensures the Shell breaks. Guardians of the Incubation.
    
- **[[LAHAT|LAHAT]] (לַהַט):** Flame / Blade. The consuming aspect of God that burns away the Ego/Shell.
    
- **[[NACHASH|NACHASH]] (נָחָשׁ):** Serpent. The voice of Duality. The Whispering Enchanter that creates the illusion of separation.
    
- **[[SATAN|SATAN]] (שָׂטָן):** Adversary. The Resistance/Friction necessary for growth.
    
- **[[AYEKAH|AYEKAH]] (אַיֶּכָּה):** "Where are you?" The Ontological Question. The Sower locating the Seed in the Soil.
    
- **[[ETZ|ETZ]] (עֵץ):** Tree. The structure of Life. The spine of connection between Heaven and Earth.
    

### THEME 4: GLORY, MANIFESTATION & REVELATION

- **[[KAVOD|KAVOD]] (כָּבוֹד):** Glory. Literally "Weight." The heavy presence of God that the vessel must be built to hold.
    
- **[[PHANEROO|PHANEROO]] (φανερόω):** Manifestation. Making visible that which was always present but hidden.
    
- **[[QAL|QAL]] (קַל):** Light / Empty. The feeling of insubstantiality (the opposite of _Kavod_) that drives the Ego to perform.
    
- **[[DA'AT|DA'AT]] (דַּעַת):** Knowledge. Experiential Union. The merging of the Knower and the Known.
    
- **[[YADAH|YADAH]] (יָדַע):** To Know. Intimacy. The verb form of union (Da'at).
    
- **[[HOMOLOGEO|HOMOLOGEO]] (ὁμολογέω):** Confession. Literally "To say the same thing." Agreeing with God's reality about you.
    

### THEME 5: THE MECHANICS OF GUIDANCE & CAPACITY

- **[[TORAH|TORAH]] (תּוֹרָה):** Instruction / Trajectory. The Mirror of Identity and the genetic code of the Seed.
    
- **[[YARAH|YARAH]] (יָרָה):** To Shoot / Cast Rain. The activation code sent to the Seed; the setting of the trajectory.
    
- **[[KADOSH|KADOSH]] (קָדוֹשׁ):** Holy / Set Apart. The "Voltage Rating." The insulation required to carry the High Current of God.
    
- **[[TAMIM|TAMIM]] (תָּמִים):** Whole / Blameless. Structural Integrity. Being undivided (Leak-proof).
    
- **[[MENUHA|MENUHA]] (מְנוּחָה):** Rest. The state of Non-Friction/Non-Striving where the Vessel docks with the Source.
    

### THEME 6: THE DISTORTION & THE RETURN

- **[[CHATA|CHATA]] (חָטָא):** Sin. Literally "To miss the mark." Failing to realize Identity; veering off the trajectory.
    
- **RA (רַע):** Evil / Dysfunctional. The breaking of relationship/function.
    
- **[[HAVEL|HAVEL]] (הֶבֶל):** Vapor / Vanity. The illusion of separation; the mist that vanishes.
    
- **[[TZAR|TZAR]] (צַר):** Narrow / Enemy. The birth canal. The pressure that feels like an enemy but leads to life.
    
- **[[SHUV|SHUV]] (שׁוּב):** Return. Repentance. Snapping back to Reality (Identity).
    
- **[[YASHA|YASHA]] (יָשַׁע):** Salvation. Liberation / Width. Being brought from the narrow place (_Tzar_) to the open place.
    
- **[[KARATH|KARATH]] (כָּרַת):** To Cut. The cutting of the Covenant. The security of the relationship.
    
- **[[RACHAMIM|RACHAMIM]] (רַחֲמִים):** Mercy. Derived from _Rechem_ (Womb). God’s Womb-Love that carries the Seed.
    
- **[[TALAH|TALAH]] (תָּלָה):** To Hang. The lifting up of the Serpent/Christ. The Judgment of the Ego displayed.
