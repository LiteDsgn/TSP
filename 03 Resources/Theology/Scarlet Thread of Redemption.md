# Scarlet Thread of Redemption

This is often referred to as the "Scarlet Thread of Redemption." In Christian theology, the Old Testament is seen as the concealed shadow of Christ, and the New Testament is the revealed substance of Christ.

Here is a list of the representation of Jesus Christ in all 66 books of the Bible, with relevant quotations.

### The Old Testament (The Shadow & Prophecy)

**1. Genesis: The Seed of the Woman** _He is the offspring who will crush the head of the serpent._

> "And I will put enmity between you and the woman, and between your offspring and hers; he will crush your head, and you will strike his heel." — **Genesis 3:15**

**2. Exodus: The Passover Lamb**_He is the sacrificial lamb whose blood protects us from judgment._

> "The blood shall be a sign for you... and when I see the blood, I will pass over you." — **Exodus 12:13**

**3. Leviticus: The High Priest**_He is the perfect High Priest making atonement for the people._

> "For the life of a creature is in the blood, and I have given it to you to make atonement for yourselves on the altar." — **Leviticus 17:11**

**4. Numbers: The Bronze Serpent / The Water from the Rock**_He is the one lifted up for our healing and the water of life._

> "Moses made a bronze snake and put it up on a pole. Then when anyone was bitten by a snake and looked at the bronze snake, they lived." — **Numbers 21:9**

**5. Deuteronomy: The Prophet Like Moses** _He is the lawgiver and the voice of God._

> "The Lord your God will raise up for you a prophet like me from among you, from your fellow Israelites. You must listen to him." — **Deuteronomy 18:15**

**6. Joshua: The Captain of the Lord’s Host** _He is the Commander of the Army of God who leads us to victory._

> "‘Neither,’ he replied, ‘but as commander of the army of the Lord I have now come.’ Then Joshua fell facedown to the ground in reverence..." — **Joshua 5:14**

**7. Judges: The Ultimate Deliverer**_He is the Judge who saves His people from their oppressors._

> "The Lord raised up for them a deliverer... who saved them." — **Judges 3:9**

**8. Ruth: The Kinsman-Redeemer**_He is the wealthy relative (Boaz) who pays the price to redeem the bride._

> "Praise be to the Lord, who this day has not left you without a guardian-redeemer." — **Ruth 4:14**

**9. 1 Samuel: The Anointed King**_He is the King God chooses, unlike the king the people chose (Saul)._

> "The Lord said, ‘Rise and anoint him; this is the one.’" — **1 Samuel 16:12**

**10. 2 Samuel: The Son of David**_He is the descendant of David whose throne will last forever._

> "I will establish the throne of his kingdom forever." — **2 Samuel 7:13**

**11. 1 Kings: The Reigning King**_He is the ruler of wisdom and glory (typified by Solomon)._

> "God gave Solomon wisdom and very great insight, and a breadth of understanding as measureless as the sand on the seashore." — **1 Kings 4:29**

**12. 2 Kings: The Miracle Worker**_He is the prophet who heals the leper and raises the dead (typified by Elisha)._

> "So he went down and dipped himself in the Jordan seven times... and his flesh was restored and became clean like that of a young boy." — **2 Kings 5:14**

**13. 1 Chronicles: The Temple Builder**_He is the one who builds the house of God._

> "He is the one who will build a house for me, and I will establish his throne forever." — **1 Chronicles 17:12**

**14. 2 Chronicles: The Preserver of the Lineage**_He is the King who preserves the royal line of David._

> "The Lord was not willing to destroy the house of David because of the covenant he had made with David." — **2 Chronicles 21:7**

**15. Ezra: The Faithful Scribe**_He is the restorer of the Temple and the Word._

> "For Ezra had devoted himself to the study and observance of the Law of the Lord." — **Ezra 7:10**

**16. Nehemiah: The Rebuilder of the Walls**_He is the restorer of what is broken in our lives._

> "Come, let us rebuild the wall of Jerusalem, and we will no longer be in disgrace." — **Nehemiah 2:17**

**17. Esther: The Advocate**_He is the one who risks His life to save His people (typified by Esther and Mordecai)._

> "And who knows but that you have come to your royal position for such a time as this?" — **Esther 4:14**

**18. Job: The Living Redeemer**_He is the one who resurrects us from the dust._

> "I know that my redeemer lives, and that in the end he will stand on the earth." — **Job 19:25**

**19. Psalms: The Good Shepherd**_He is the song of our hearts and the Shepherd of our souls._

> "The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing." — **Psalm 23:1**

**20. Proverbs: The Wisdom of God**_He is the personification of divine wisdom._

> "The Lord brought me forth as the first of his works, before his deeds of old." — **Proverbs 8:22**

**21. Ecclesiastes: The Meaning of Life**_He is the purpose beyond the "vanity" of life under the sun._

> "Fear God and keep his commandments, for this is the duty of all mankind." — **Ecclesiastes 12:13**

**22. Song of Solomon: The Bridegroom**_He is the lover of our souls._

> "My beloved is mine and I am his; he browses among the lilies." — **Song of Solomon 2:16**

**23. Isaiah: The Prince of Peace**_He is the Suffering Servant and the Ruling King._

> "For to us a child is born... And he will be called Wonderful Counselor, Mighty God, Everlasting Father, Prince of Peace." — **Isaiah 9:6**

**24. Jeremiah: The Righteous Branch**_He is the new covenant written on our hearts._

> "I will raise up for David a righteous Branch, a King who will reign wisely." — **Jeremiah 23:5**

**25. Lamentations: The Weeping Prophet**_He is the one who weeps over the sins of His people._

> "Because of the Lord’s great love we are not consumed, for his compassions never fail." — **Lamentations 3:22**

**26. Ezekiel: The Son of Man**_He is the River of Life flowing from the Temple._

> "Swarms of living creatures will live wherever the river flows... so where the river flows everything will live." — **Ezekiel 47:9**

**27. Daniel: The Fourth Man in the Fire**_He is the one who stands with us in the fire of tribulation._

> "Look! I see four men walking around in the fire, unbound and unharmed, and the fourth looks like a son of the gods." — **Daniel 3:25**

**28. Hosea: The Faithful Husband**_He is the groom who redeems his unfaithful bride._

> "I will betroth you to me forever; I will betroth you in righteousness and justice, in love and compassion." — **Hosea 2:19**

**29. Joel: The Baptizer with the Holy Spirit**_He is the pourer of the Spirit on all flesh._

> "And afterward, I will pour out my Spirit on all people." — **Joel 2:28**

**30. Amos: The Burden Bearer**_He is the judge of nations and the restorer of Israel._

> "In that day I will restore David’s fallen shelter." — **Amos 9:11**

**31. Obadiah: The Mighty Savior**_He is the Judge of the proud and the Savior of the humble._

> "Deliverers will go up on Mount Zion to govern the mountains of Esau. And the kingdom will be the Lord’s." — **Obadiah 1:21**

**32. Jonah: The Risen Prophet**_He is the sign of the resurrection (three days in the belly of the earth)._

> "But the Lord provided a huge fish to swallow Jonah, and Jonah was in the belly of the fish three days and three nights." — **Jonah 1:17**

**33. Micah: The Ruler from Bethlehem**_He is the Ancient of Days born in a small village._

> "But you, Bethlehem Ephrathah... out of you will come for me one who will be ruler over Israel." — **Micah 5:2**

**34. Nahum: The Stronghold in the Day of Trouble**_He is the Avenger of God’s people._

> "The Lord is good, a refuge in times of trouble. He cares for those who trust in him." — **Nahum 1:7**

**35. Habakkuk: The God of My Salvation**_He is the joy in the midst of famine._

> "Yet I will rejoice in the Lord, I will be joyful in God my Savior." — **Habakkuk 3:18**

**36. Zephaniah: The King in Our Midst**_He is the mighty warrior who saves and sings over us._

> "The Lord your God is with you, the Mighty Warrior who saves. He will take great delight in you." — **Zephaniah 3:17**

**37. Haggai: The Desire of All Nations**_He is the glory that fills the temple._

> "I will shake all nations, and what is desired by all nations will come, and I will fill this house with glory." — **Haggai 2:7**

**38. Zechariah: The Pierced Branch**_He is the King riding on a donkey and the fountain for sin._

> "They will look on me, the one they have pierced, and they will mourn for him as one mourns for an only child." — **Zechariah 12:10**

**39. Malachi: The Sun of Righteousness**_He is the refiner’s fire and the healer._

> "But for you who revere my name, the sun of righteousness will rise with healing in its rays." — **Malachi 4:2**

### The New Testament (The Reality & Fulfillment)

**40. Matthew: The King of the Jews**_He is the promised Messiah of the Davidic line._

> "Where is the one who has been born king of the Jews? We saw his star when it rose and have come to worship him." — **Matthew 2:2**

**41. Mark: The Suffering Servant**_He is the wonder-worker who came to serve, not be served._

> "For even the Son of Man did not come to be served, but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many." — **Mark 10:45**

**42. Luke: The Son of Man**_He is the perfect man who seeks the lost._

> "For the Son of Man came to seek and to save the lost." — **Luke 19:10**

**43. John: The Son of God**_He is the Word made flesh._

> "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God... The Word became flesh." — **John 1:1, 14**

**44. Acts: The Ascended Lord**_He is the One sending the Holy Spirit to the Church._

> "Exalted to the right hand of God, he has received from the Father the promised Holy Spirit and has poured out what you now see and hear." — **Acts 2:33**

**45. Romans: The Justifier**_He is the righteousness of God given to us._

> "Therefore, since we have been justified through faith, we have peace with God through our Lord Jesus Christ." — **Romans 5:1**

**46. 1 Corinthians: The Firstfruits**_He is the power of God and the first to rise from the dead._

> "But Christ has indeed been raised from the dead, the firstfruits of those who have fallen asleep." — **1 Corinthians 15:20**

**47. 2 Corinthians: The Comforter**_He is the power made perfect in weakness._

> "My grace is sufficient for you, for my power is made perfect in weakness." — **2 Corinthians 12:9**

**48. Galatians: The Liberator**_He is the end of the law and the giver of freedom._

> "It is for freedom that Christ has set us free." — **Galatians 5:1**

**49. Ephesians: The Head of the Church**_He is the cornerstone of the whole building._

> "And God placed all things under his feet and appointed him to be head over everything for the church." — **Ephesians 1:22**

**50. Philippians: The Name Above All Names**_He is the supplier of our joy and needs._

> "Therefore God exalted him to the highest place and gave him the name that is above every name." — **Philippians 2:9**

**51. Colossians: The Fullness of the Godhead**_He is the image of the invisible God._

> "For in Christ all the fullness of the Deity lives in bodily form." — **Colossians 2:9**

**52. 1 Thessalonians: The Coming King**_He is the one who will return for His church (The Rapture)._

> "For the Lord himself will come down from heaven... and the dead in Christ will rise first." — **1 Thessalonians 4:16**

**53. 2 Thessalonians: The Returning Judge**_He is the one who will overthrow the lawless one._

> "And then the lawless one will be revealed, whom the Lord Jesus will overthrow with the breath of his mouth." — **2 Thessalonians 2:8**

**54. 1 Timothy: The Mediator**_He is the bridge between God and Man._

> "For there is one God and one mediator between God and mankind, the man Christ Jesus." — **1 Timothy 2:5**

**55. 2 Timothy: The Bestower of Crowns**_He is the righteous Judge who rewards the faithful._

> "Now there is in store for me the crown of righteousness, which the Lord, the righteous Judge, will award to me on that day." — **2 Timothy 4:8**

**56. Titus: The Great God and Savior**_He is the blessed hope._

> "While we wait for the blessed hope—the appearing of the glory of our great God and Savior, Jesus Christ." — **Titus 2:13**

**57. Philemon: The Payer of Our Debt**_He is the Friend closer than a brother who pays what we owe._

> "If he has done you any wrong or owes you anything, charge it to me." — **Philemon 1:18** (Paul typifying Christ's intercession).

**58. Hebrews: The Great High Priest**_He is the blood that speaks better things than Abel._

> "Therefore, since we have a great high priest who has ascended into heaven, Jesus the Son of God, let us hold firmly to the faith we profess." — **Hebrews 4:14**

**59. James: The Lord of Glory**_He is the Healer of the sick._

> "My believers in our glorious Lord Jesus Christ must not show favoritism." — **James 2:1**

**60. 1 Peter: The Chief Shepherd**_He is the precious cornerstone._

> "And when the Chief Shepherd appears, you will receive the crown of glory that will never fade away." — **1 Peter 5:4**

**61. 2 Peter: The Day Star**_He is the morning star rising in our hearts._

> "...until the day dawns and the morning star rises in your hearts." — **2 Peter 1:19**

**62. 1 John: The Advocate**_He is the life and the love of God._

> "But if anybody does sin, we have an advocate with the Father—Jesus Christ, the Righteous One." — **1 John 2:1**

**63. 2 John: The Truth**_He is the truth that dwells in us forever._

> "...because of the truth, which lives in us and will be with us forever." — **2 John 1:2**

**64. 3 John: The Way**_He is the pattern of goodness._

> "Dear friend, do not imitate what is evil but what is good. Anyone who does what is good is from God." — **3 John 1:11**

**65. Jude: The Keeper**_He is the one who keeps us from falling._

> "To him who is able to keep you from stumbling and to present you before his glorious presence without fault and with great joy." — **Jude 1:24**

**66. Revelation: The King of Kings and Lord of Lords**_He is the Alpha and the Omega._

> "On his robe and on his thigh he has this name written: KING OF KINGS AND LORD OF LORDS." — **Revelation 19:16**
