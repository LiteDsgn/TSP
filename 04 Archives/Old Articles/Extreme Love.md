# Extreme Love

God's love is extreme and excessively elaborate

  

God's love goes beyond every reason you have for him not loving you

  

God's love goes beyond necessity. It lacks moderation, balance and restraint.

  

God's love is not cautious but extravagant.

  

God is not jealous because of you. He is jealous of you.

  

He's not jealous because we have other interests in life.
